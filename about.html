About Scaries

Scaries is a recurring live-band karaoke event where audience members take the stage backed by a professional house band.

The Event

The band performs full arrangements of pop, rock, emo, punk, indie, and alternative favorites.

Part concert, part karaoke, part community gathering, Scaries turns a typical night into a sing-along party where first-time singers, local musicians, and neighborhood regulars share the same stage.

Unlike traditional karaoke, every song is performed live by the band, creating an experience that feels somewhere between a rock show and an open mic.

The History

Scaries began in a backyard in Bushwick as a semi-regular potluck and live-music hang where friends, local musicians, and adventurous singers would gather to call everything from Backstreet Boys and show tunes to classic rock, emo, pop-punk, and the occasional wildly ambitious deep cut.

The atmosphere was equal parts rehearsal, party, and happy accident. Sometimes it ended with a restart from the top, other times with spontaneous three-part harmonies appearing out of nowhere.

After a few noise complaints and a growing crowd, the event found an unlikely champion in Baker Falls owner Nick Bodor during the venue's reopening. What began as an occasional experiment soon became a recurring fixture at Baker Falls, growing into a bi-monthly event that has welcomed hundreds of singers to the stage and built a loyal community of regulars, musicians, and first-timers alike.
